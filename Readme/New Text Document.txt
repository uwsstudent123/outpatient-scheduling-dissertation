# Data Driven Appointment Scheduling for Hospital Management

This repository contains the Google Colab notebook, synthetic dataset,
and figures used in the MSc dissertation project.

## Contents
- notebook/ : Google Colab notebook containing all analysis, models, and simulation
- data/     : synthetic master dataset (CSV)
- figures/  : Charts Graphs, Table and PNGs used in Chapters 4 and 5 of the dissertation

## Notes
- All analysis is performed in the Colab notebook.
- All data is synthetic and anonymised.
- No real patient or hospital data is used.
- Results reproduce the KPIs and figures reported in the dissertation.
